THE QUESTIONS EVERY MD ASKS

"How do we know you won't approach our clients?" +

We operate under NDA and non-solicitation clauses as standard. Your client relationship is yours — in conversation and in writing. Our business model depends on agencies trusting us with repeat work, not on burning bridges for a single project.

"What if the quality doesn't match our standards?" +

Every Extra partner is a proven specialist — not a generalist on a good day. Before we accept any brief, we assess whether we have the exact match. If we don't, we say so upfront rather than compromising. You'll see the work in progress, not just at final delivery.

"Can we justify the cost and still make margin?" +

Our pricing sits between freelancer rates and agency premiums. Run it against the cost of a permanent specialist hire, or against the cost of turning down the brief entirely. In most cases, Extra is the highest-margin option available for specialist work you can't deliver in-house.
